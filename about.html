Who We Are

Bridging the Information Gap in Healthcare

While existing healthcare platforms help users find doctors and book appointments, patients often remain unaware of actual clinic conditions — such as waiting time, patient load, and service availability. This lack of visibility frequently results in long waits, uncertainty, and inefficient healthcare experiences.

CareFlow addresses this challenge by introducing Queue Intelligence — enabling patients to view real-time clinic information before visiting a healthcare provider. By offering insights such as estimated waiting time, patient queue status, and clinic workload, CareFlow empowers users to make informed decisions and better manage their time.
